App review readiness for every target app.

SubmitPilot is a native Mac workspace for developers preparing apps for App Store submission. Build a focused audit, collect evidence, export a readiness report, and draft App Review notes before submission day.

Built for pre-submission work

Target app audits

Create separate readiness workspaces for the apps you plan to submit, with bundle ID, platform, category, version, reviewer access, and risk tags in one place.

Guided checks

Review product completeness, metadata, privacy, permission prompts, StoreKit, account access, and App Review notes with status and notes for each item.

Evidence vault

Collect screenshots, links, local file references, and review credentials evidence next to the checklist they support.

Local-first launch QA

SubmitPilot helps organize your own app-submission process. It does not submit apps to Apple, guarantee approval, impersonate Apple, or bypass App Review.

Use it as a repeatable workspace for the checks and handoff notes that tend to get scattered across spreadsheets, issue trackers, chats, and screenshots.

Private by default

SubmitPilot runs locally on your Mac, does not require an account, and does not use analytics or tracking. Audit data stays on your Mac unless you choose to export or share it.
